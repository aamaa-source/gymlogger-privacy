# Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

GymLogger X respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

GymLogger X does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

All workout data and preferences are stored locally on your device.

## Subscriptions

Subscriptions and payments are handled by Apple through the App Store. GymLogger X does not receive or store any payment information.

## Analytics

GymLogger X does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.

## Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with any third party.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. When updated, the date at the top will be revised.
